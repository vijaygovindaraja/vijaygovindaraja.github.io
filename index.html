About Me

I specialize in distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and systems performance. My research interests include privacy-preserving healthcare systems, federated learning for clinical applications, and medical AI diagnostics.

I act as a technical owner and decision-maker for complex initiatives, leading zero-to-one efforts and guiding cross-functional teams. I balance hands-on system design and implementation with mentorship, stakeholder alignment, and delivery of durable, production-grade solutions.

I serve as Associate Editor for IEEE Internet of Things Journal and npj Digital Medicine (Nature Portfolio). My published work spans federated learning, medical imaging, IoT security, and cloud systems.
